Working with me

For each design I charge an hourly rate, every time the artwork comes back to me you will be charged for changes. Once you are happy with print ready files, you'd signoff the final artwork and I will get you intouch with a printer or you can go to your own printer. If we are developing a website together, once you are happy with the final prototype and signoff of the visuals. I'd supply you with all the necessary files and email you a hosting plan or you can find your own site to host. Whenever you require the site to be updated, I will charge a separate fee again and we go through the same process again. If we are developing interfaces and apps, we meet and brainstorm, then I would supply you with wireframes and prototypes, they're after you'd supply them to your developer. Sadly at this point in time I don't know a good developer, so you'd have to find someone whose good at writing code.
