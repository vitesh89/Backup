Alittle about me

"my little backstory"

I was born in Durban South Africa and from a very young age I showed an interest in drawing. I'd spend soo much time drawing it would often land me in trouble, but I still continued later I started messing around with images on photoshop and creating my own wierd posters and artistic pieces.

In 2011 I joined the print industry which introduced me to the lithographic printing process and I absorbed as much as I could, then snow balled into graphic design and now web and UI/UX design.
